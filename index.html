Our Mission

Why Art Made Easy?

We believe art should never feel exclusive. Here's what guides us.

  • 🚪
    Art is for Everyone Art has historically been discussed in complex, academic language that can feel unwelcoming. We change that by using plain, everyday words without dumbing anything down.
  • 📖
    Simplified for Beginners Whether you've never visited a museum or you're a curious student, our guides meet you exactly where you are, no prior knowledge required.
  • ♿
    Built with Accessibility in Mind Every page follows accessibility best practices, clear contrast, readable fonts, image descriptions, and keyboard-friendly navigation, so no one is left out.
Our Courses

Two Movements to Explore

We're focusing on two of the most influential art movements in Western history. Pick a movement below to start learning.

I

Baroque

Bold. Dramatic. Intense. Baroque art (roughly 1600–1750) used extreme light and shadow to create paintings full of emotion, movement, and religious meaning. Think candlelit scenes where every shadow tells a story.

Start Lesson →
II

Impressionism

Soft. Hazy. Beautiful. Impressionism (1860s–1880s) broke the rules of precise painting and instead captured light, color, and everyday moments with quick, loose brushstrokes, like a memory painted in sunlight.
