Making Art History Approachable for Everyone

Art history has a reputation for being difficult and exclusive. This platform changes that, two major movements, explained in plain language, with real artworks from the Modern Art Museum of Fort Worth.

Why Art
Made Easy?

  • 01
    Art is for Everyone

    Art history has long been discussed in language that feels unwelcoming to anyone outside a university setting. We write for real people, using plain words without losing the substance.

  • 02
    Simplified for Beginners

    Whether you have never visited a museum or you are a student looking for a starting point, these guides meet you where you are. No art background required.

  • 03
    Built with Accessibility in Mind

    Every page is designed with strong contrast, readable text, image descriptions, and keyboard-friendly navigation so that no one is excluded from learning.

Two Movements to Explore

c. 1600 - 1750

Baroque

Dramatic light and shadow, intense emotion, and deep religious meaning. Baroque painting was meant to move you.

c. 1860s - 1880s

Impressionism

Loose brushstrokes, captured light, and ordinary life as subject matter. The Impressionists changed what painting was allowed to be.
